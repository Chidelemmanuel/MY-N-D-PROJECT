About Us

Welcome to Orbit Hotel & Suites,your ultimate destination for luxury accommodation in the bustling city of Onitsha. Our hotel is strategically loacted in the heart of the city, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure travellers.


At Orbit Hotel and Suites, we are committed to providing our guests with exceptional service and world-class amenities. Our well-appointed rooms and suites are designed with your comfort in mind, featuring plush bedding, modern furnishings and state of the art technology


Our hotel boasts a range of facilities to ensure your stay is both enjoyable and productive. Whether you're looking to relax by the pool, stay fit in our fully-equipped gym, or indulge in a delicious meal at our restaurant, we have something for everyone.


Our friendly and attentive staffare always on hand to assist with any requests you may have, and our 24-hours reception ensures that you can always reach us whenever you need us.


We pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability and we have implemented various initiatives to minimize our environmental impact. From energy-efficient lighting to respponsible waste management, we strive to make a positive contribution to our community and the planet.
